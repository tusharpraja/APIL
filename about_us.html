Company Profile

Standing firm on the pillars of integrity, innovation and excellence, Edgetech is driven by the passion to provide clean, breathable air to the world. With a commitment to reduce the planet’s energy footprint by contributing towards a sustainable environment for our present and future generations, Edgetech was established in 2005 under the visionary tutelage of founders Mr RP Goel and his son Mr Amit Goel. Guided by their enriching experience of more than four decades in the industry, the organisation has grown from strength to strength specializing in the end-to-end production of advanced quality, next generation Air Handling Systems.

Edgetech harnesses advanced technology fuelled by innovation across its products and processes. Stringent quality control, continuous upgradation of technology, high quality equipment and efficiency focussed techniques have enabled us to create an enviable range of award-winning products for all kinds of HVAC applications. Offering the widest range of air handling solutions and its allied products in the country, Edgetech has carved a niche in the industry with its passion to manufacture best in class products. Ignited by commitment and an unflinching faith in the power of ‘Make in India’, the organisation has charted outstanding milestones in its incredible journey.

Untiring efforts, relentless research and a customer-centric approach empowers us to deliver best in class solutions mapped to the demands of the continuously evolving industry. At Edgetech, it is our earnest mission and vision to be the leading brand in the HVAC business across the globe kindled by engineering excellence and reinforced by strong practices for a sustainable environment. We are out on a mission to serve humanity by providing the most technologically advanced air handling solutions across the globe.

We are solution providers

We are not just manufacturers; we offer comprehensive and innovative air handling products and solutions for HVAC industry. We provide end-to-end services encompassing design, component selection, manufacturing, assembly, delivery, onsite installation and after sales service. Edgetech specializes in the manufacture of premium air handling systems and its allied products for a wide spectrum of applications covering commercial spaces and buildings, healthcare, hospitality, educational institutions, manufacturing industries, pharmaceutical industry, IT and Telecommunication sector, commercial kitchens, railways, malls, automobiles, residentials and many more

Our highly qualified and dedicated team of engineers and marketing professionals ensure that we deliver world class systems from start to finish. Our years of understanding of customer needs and expectations supported by our mission to offer the best possible cost-effective solutions, give us an added edge. We are a one-stop solution provider for the air handling industry.

We Make in India, For the World

Our sophisticated best-in-class class products are manufactured in India and delivered across the world. We ensure each of our products is at par with global quality standards through continuous infrastructure expansion and technology upgradation. Our state-of-the-art infrastructure, well integrated with world class equipment and tech savvy processes enables us to manufacture air-handling systems of energy-optimized design, tailored to specific applications and geared for minimized power consumption. With the most extensive delivery network in the country connected to the remotest corners, our robust supply chain caters to pan India. Our advanced quality products at par with global standards have helped us create a valuable consumer base across Indian subcontinent and other parts of the world. Backed by innovation and cutting edge technology, it is our persistent drive to create sustainable solutions not just for India, but for the world.

What makes us different

From our inception in 2005 till date, we are driven by our distinctive E3 Edge philosophy based on the trinity of Energy Efficiency, Eco Friendliness & Engineering Excellence. With environmental sustainability as our core value, we dedicate our efforts towards reducing carbon emissions and energy cost in all our products and processes. Technological advancements, stringent quality control and finest equipment ensure premium quality products across our entire range. We have many firsts to our credit – along with AHUs, Edgetech is the first company in India to be renowned for AHRI certified FCUs. Spread across 1.5 lakh sq. ft, our state-of-the-art modern facility at Rohad, Haryana, is an IGBC certified platinum-rated manufacturing facility, one of India’s first in the HVAC industry with a manufacturing capacity of 30,000 AHUs & 24,000 FCUs annually. Our strength lies in our indigenous manufacturing units, relentless pursuit of innovation and environment friendliness checks at every stage of production. Added to this, our manufacturing mechanism provides for maximum flexibility in dimensions, materials & components, making the entire process customer oriented. We take pride in having the fastest delivery network in the country and the best after sales setup in India as in other countries. What makes us truly unique is our provision for onsite testing of all our products while adhering to the strictest quality checks, for we believe in a comfortable, hurdle-free delivery and smooth installation with customer satisfaction as our prime focus. At Edgetech, we are focused on creating a seamless experience for all our clients.
